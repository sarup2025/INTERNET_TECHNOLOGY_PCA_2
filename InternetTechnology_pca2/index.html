MAULANA ABUL KALAM AZAD UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology, is a public state university located in Kalyani, West Bengal, India. It is funded completely by the Government of West Bengal. It was established in 2001 by the West Bengal legislature.
